lingvo.core.enhancer module¶

An enhancer optimizes a subset of variables according to a loss.

It consists of a learning rate schedule, an optimizer, and gradient clipping mechanisms. A BaseTask can have multiple enhancers, each optimizing a (usually disjoint) subset of variables.
